Privacy Policy

MindFlow Studio

Last updated: April 3, 2026

1. Introduction

LS-D Consulting SASU ("we", "our", or "us") operates MindFlow Studio. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

Information from TikTok:

  • TikTok user ID and username
  • Profile information (name, avatar)
  • Access tokens for publishing content
  • Video analytics data (views, likes, engagement)

Information You Provide:

  • Email address (if provided)
  • Content preferences and settings
  • Feedback and support requests

Automatically Collected Information:

  • Usage data (features used, frequency)
  • Device information (browser type, OS)
  • IP address and location data
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Generate and publish content to your TikTok account
  • Analyze performance and optimize content strategy
  • Communicate with you about the Service
  • Improve and develop new features
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. AI-Generated Content

We use AI services to generate content:

  • Claude (Anthropic): Generates video scripts based on trends and your niche
  • Veo (Google): Generates cinematic B-roll videos
  • ElevenLabs: Generates voice-overs
  • AssemblyAI: Generates captions

Content prompts and generated outputs may be processed by these third-party AI services. We do not share personally identifiable information with these services.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We share your information with:

  • TikTok: To publish content on your behalf (via OAuth authorization)
  • AI Service Providers: For content generation (anonymized prompts)
  • Infrastructure Providers: Railway, Google Cloud, n8n (for hosting and automation)
  • Analytics Services: To understand usage patterns (anonymized data)

We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

6. Data Retention

  • TikTok access tokens: Until you revoke authorization
  • Generated content: 30 days after publication
  • Analytics data: 12 months
  • Account information: Until account deletion

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate information
  • Erasure: Delete your account and associated data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke TikTok authorization at any time

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure API authentication (OAuth 2.0)
  • Access controls and logging
  • Regular security audits

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. TikTok Data Usage

We access your TikTok account solely to:

  • Publish videos on your behalf
  • Retrieve analytics for content optimization

We do NOT:

  • Access your TikTok messages or private data
  • Share your TikTok data with third parties for marketing
  • Use your data for purposes other than providing the Service

10. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Maintain your session
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze usage patterns

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

11. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

12. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Email notification
  • Prominent notice on the Service
  • Updating the "Last updated" date

14. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you are in the European Union, you have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to restriction of processing
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority
